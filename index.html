👽User Manual

What is MindOrbit?

A fractal mind-map where your ideas orbit each other. Tap any node to focus it, tap again to edit.

Node Types

☀️ Me — your centre · 💡 Idea — orbits Me · ✅ Step — orbits an Idea · 📦 Module — orbits a Step. All names are customisable in Settings.

Navigation

Drag canvas to pan · Pinch / scroll to zoom · Tap a node to focus · Tap focused node to edit · + NodeName button at the bottom to add a child.

Home & Freeze

Tap ☀️ to shuffle the fractal layout. Enable ❄️ Freeze to pin every node — drag freely, children follow their parent. Press 💾 to commit positions as your permanent Home. While frozen, ☀️ restores your saved Home.

Finance

Each node has optional Budget / Cost (money out) and Expected / Income (money in) fields. Tap ＋ Finance to reveal them. The P&L row appears automatically when both sides are filled. Tap 💰 for a full overview.

Data & Backup

All data is stored locally (localStorage). Use Export JSON to back up — it includes everything: nodes, positions, icons, finance data, glow colours, and your saved Home layout. Import JSON restores the full state on any device. Export regularly; browsers can clear local storage.

Installation (PWA)

MindOrbit installs as a native-like app on any device — no app store needed. On Android (Chrome): tap 📲 Install App in Settings or the banner at the bottom. On iOS (Safari): tap ↑ Share then «Add to Home Screen». On desktop: look for the install icon in the browser address bar. Once installed, MindOrbit works fully offline — all your data stays on your device.

Alle Daten werden lokal gespeichert (localStorage). Verwenden Sie JSON exportieren zur Sicherung — es enthält alles: Knoten, Positionen, Icons, Finanzdaten, Leuchtfarben und Ihr gespeichertes Startseiten-Layout. JSON importieren stellt den vollständigen Zustand auf jedem Gerät wieder her.

Installation (PWA)

Auf iOS (Safari): Tippen Sie auf ↑ Teilen dann «Zum Home-Bildschirm». Auf dem Desktop: Installations-Symbol in der Adressleiste suchen. Nach der Installation funktioniert MindOrbit vollständig offline.
